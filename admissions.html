Admissions

Welcome to the admissions page of Mountsview Technical College. We are excited to help you start your journey towards a rewarding education.

Application Process

  1. Complete the online application form.
  2. Submit required documents.
  3. Pay the application fee.
  4. Schedule an interview (if applicable).
  5. Receive your admission decision.

Admission Requirements

  • High school diploma or equivalent.
  • Completed application form.
  • Official transcripts.
  • Personal statement.
  • Letters of recommendation (optional).

Financial Aid

Mountsview Technical College offers various financial aid options to help students afford their education. Visit our financial aid page for more information.
