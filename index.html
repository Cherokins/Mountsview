Our Courses

Industry-relevant programs designed for your success

Information Technology

Digital Literacy/Computer Packages

Master essential computer skills including Microsoft Office, internet usage, and basic computer operations.

Graphic Design

Learn professional design tools like Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and CorelDraw for print and digital media.

Computer Repairs

Gain hands-on skills in hardware troubleshooting, software installation, and computer maintenance.

Technical Programs

Fashion Design

Learn garment construction, pattern making, textile selection, and fashion illustration techniques.

Hair Dressing and Beauty

Master hair styling, treatments, makeup application, and salon management skills.

Electronics Repair

Develop skills in diagnosing and repairing electronic devices including TVs, radios, and home appliances.

Electrical Installation

Learn wiring, circuit design, and electrical system installation for residential and commercial buildings.

CNA/HSS

Peri-Operative Theatre Technology

Phlebotomy

Community Health

Training in public health education, disease prevention, and community outreach.

Nutrition and Dietetics

Study food science, meal planning, and nutritional counseling for health and wellness.
