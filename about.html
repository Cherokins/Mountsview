About Mountsview Technical College

Mountsview Technical College is dedicated to providing hands-on, practical, and industry-relevant education in Kenya. Our mission is to empower students through skills-based training, experienced teaching staff, and certifications recognized across the nation. We offer diverse programs in Health Sciences, Information Technology, and Technical Trades, equipping students for meaningful careers.
