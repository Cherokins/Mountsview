About Mountsview Technical College

Mountsview Technical College is committed to providing practical, career-focused education in Kenya. Our mission is to empower students through skills-based training, experienced teaching staff, and nationally recognized certifications. We offer a range of programs in IT, technical trades, and health sciences.
