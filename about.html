Our Mission

Our Vision

To become East Africa’s leading institution in vocational and technical training excellence.

Our History

Mountsview Technical College was founded in Naro Moru with a strong belief in transforming communities through education. Over the years, we’ve trained professionals who now work across Kenya in sectors ranging from healthcare to engineering.
