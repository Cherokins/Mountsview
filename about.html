About Mountsview Technical College

Discover our rich history, mission, and state-of-the-art facilities

Our History

Mountsview Technical College has grown from a small vocational training center to one of Kenya's leading institutions for technical and health sciences education. Our journey began with just three programs and 50 students, and today we offer over TVETA accredited programs to more than 1,200 students annually.

Over the years, we've continuously adapted our curriculum to meet industry demands, ensuring our graduates remain competitive in the job market. Our alumni network now spans across Kenya and beyond, with graduates making significant contributions in various technical and healthcare fields.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

To provide quality technical and health sciences education that equips students with practical skills, knowledge, and professional ethics for personal development and national growth.

Vision

To be the premier technical college in Kenya recognized for excellence in skills training, innovation, and graduate employability.

Core Values

  • Excellence in Training
  • Innovation
  • Integrity
  • Professionalism
  • Student-Centered Approach
