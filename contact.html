Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Admission requirements vary by program. Generally, certificate programs require a minimum KCSE mean grade of D (plain), while diploma programs require C- (minus). Mature entry is available for applicants over 25 with relevant experience. See our Admissions page for full details.

We have three main intakes per year with the following application deadlines: January intake (December 15), May intake (April 15), and September intake (August 20). Late applications may be considered if spaces are available.

Yes, we have hostel accommodation available at KES 15,000 per term. Spaces are limited and allocated on a first-come, first-served basis. Off-campus housing assistance is also available for students who prefer private accommodation.

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans where students can pay 50% of fees on admission and the balance in monthly installments. Special arrangements can be made for students facing financial difficulties.

Our career services include resume writing workshops, interview preparation, job placement assistance, and regular career fairs. We also maintain strong industry partnerships to facilitate internships and employment opportunities for our graduates.
