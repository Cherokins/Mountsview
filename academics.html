Admissions Process

Your journey to technical education starts here

How to Apply

Follow these simple steps to join Mountsview Technical College

1

Choose Your Program

Browse our academic programs and select the course that aligns with your career goals.

2

Check Requirements

Ensure you meet the minimum entry requirements for your chosen program (listed below).

3

Submit Application

Complete the application process through your preferred method (online, WhatsApp, or in-person).

4

Receive Admission Letter

Successful applicants will receive an official admission letter within 7 working days.

5

Pay Fees & Register

Complete fee payment and registration to secure your place in the program.

Entry Requirements

Certificate Programs

  • Minimum KCSE mean grade of D (plain) or equivalent
  • KCSE certificate or equivalent qualification
  • National ID or birth certificate
  • 2 passport-size photos

Diploma Programs

  • Minimum KCSE mean grade of C- (minus) or equivalent
  • KCSE certificate or equivalent qualification
  • National ID or birth certificate
  • 2 passport-size photos
  • Relevant certificate for upgrade students

Mature Entry

  • Minimum age of 25 years
  • Relevant work experience in chosen field
  • Pass mature entry examination
